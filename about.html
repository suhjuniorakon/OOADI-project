About PassPortal

At ICT University, we believe in innovation that enhances the student experience. Our Exam Pass Collection System is a digital platform designed to simplify and secure the process of accessing examination halls.

Students who have paid their school fees can now easily upload their receipts to our website. Once verified, the system automatically generates a personalized exam pass, reducing administrative delays and giving students immediate access to their exam credentials.

This project was made possible through the collaborative efforts of dedicated developers, UI/UX designers, and system analysts—students and staff working together to streamline university operations.

We are proud to present a system that saves time, reduces errors, and supports a smoother academic process for everyone at ICT University.
